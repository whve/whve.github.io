## The Long Game of Habits

Maintaining a healthy routine is a **long-term commitment**. Over the past few months, I’ve focused on small, consistent practices that build momentum without burning out.

- Wake up at a consistent time
- Keep mornings low-friction
- Prioritize rest as much as work

## Minimalism in Daily Life

A **minimalist mindset** helps reduce decision fatigue. By removing clutter and keeping only what matters, the day becomes clearer:

- Fewer subscriptions, more focus
- A small set of productivity tools
- Clear boundaries on screen time

## How I Stay Accountable

Consistency is easier when systems support it. I use simple checkpoints:

- A weekly review of goals
- A short daily journal entry
- A monthly “health check” on sleep and energy

Small daily actions compound. By valuing **regular rhythm over dramatic bursts**, I stay aligned with long-term goals.
