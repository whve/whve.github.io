## Migration Story

In late 2024, I migrated notes from an old note app into a new Quarto-backed archive. The process was a mix of **clean-up, categorization, and preservation**.

- Exported notes as markdown where possible
- Tagged by topic and date
- Archived what felt outdated, kept what still adds value

## What Stayed Valuable

Not everything needed to move. The most useful items were:

- Short lessons learned from projects
- Snippets of ideas that still resonate
- Timeless references and frameworks

## The Role of Archives

An archive is not just a dump; it’s a **living memory**. I revisit it occasionally to:

- Refresh long-term perspective
- Reuse past thinking in new contexts
- Celebrate progress without getting stuck in nostalgia

This migration was an important step in keeping history accessible and making space for new work.
