## Curating the Collection

I started a small **music resource library** focused on the golden era of 70s–90s pop and traditional folk songs like “Yimeng Mountain Melody” (《沂蒙山小调》).

- Gather recordings in lossless quality when possible
- Note key performances and historical context
- Link to lyric translations and stories

## Why This Era Matters

This period produced rich melodies and **deep cultural resonance**. Pop stars and folk singers alike captured everyday life and deeper values:

- Classic pop with memorable hooks
- Folk songs rooted in places and people
- Bridging generations through music

## How I Use the Resource

I keep the library as a creative reference and as a source of inspiration:

- Play tracks during focused work sessions
- Use lyrics as writing prompts
- Share gems with friends to spark conversation

This resource is both a nostalgia lab and a source of **ongoing inspiration**, reminding me why music can be a powerful part of a knowledge system.
